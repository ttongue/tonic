IMPORTANT NEXT STEPS:

You've taken the first important step toward becoming a member of the Tech Valley Center of Gravity, but you need to complete the following steps to finish the new member process:

  • New members MUST complete the mandatory safety training class prior to working in the makerspace for liability reasons. Please view the green tool safety video here, and then take the quiz.
  • Once the safety training quiz is complete, you can request an access control fob to let you into the Center of Gravity using the access fob request form. This form requires a good quality headshot (think passport!) for our records for identification purposes.
  • If you'd like to have training on the equipment, there is a series of events titled "Zero To Maker" that is run to help new members learn the basics of laser cutting, 3D printing, wood shop and other areas. Please check our online calendar for an up-to-date schedule.
  • If you have not already done so, please join the Facebook page to keep in touch with other members, and check the calendar frequently for new events!

Thank you for becoming a part of the Tech Valley Center of Gravity!
